Supplement Path (Medigap)

The primary way to cover the federal 20% coinsurance and deductibles. Keeps Original Medicare as primary.

  • ✓ Zero/Low Copays at time of service
  • ✓ See any Medicare doctor nationwide
  • ✓ No referrals or networks required

Advantage Path (Part C)

Private managed care (HMO/PPO) that replaces Original Medicare and bundles coverage into a managed system.

  • ✓ Low or $0 Monthly Premiums
  • ✓ Includes Dental, Vision, and Hearing
  • ✓ Annual Out-of-Pocket Maximum Safety Net
